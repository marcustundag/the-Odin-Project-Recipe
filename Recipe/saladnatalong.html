Salad Na Talong

Ingrients
  • 2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 cup cane vinegAR
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 thumb size ginger - finely chopped
  • 1 small size red onion - chopped
  • 3 pcs medium size eggplant
  • chili (optional) depends on how spicy you want on the dish

  1. In an open flame (you can use your stove directly), grill the eggplant until its skin turns black. Make sure to not remove the stalk yet as it will make it easy for you to turn the vegetable and how an even cook on each side.

  2. hen soak in water to cool, and carefully remove the burnt skin. Rinse with water.

  3. Cut the stalks of the eggplant and cut it into 3 inches length and set aside.

  4. n a bowl, add the vinegar, sugar, salt, ginger, and red onions. Mix until the sugar and salt are thoroughly dissolved. Once dissolved, add the coconut milk and stir for another minute.

  5. Mix the eggplant in the dressing until all the ingredients are incorporated.

  6. Serve and enjoy!
